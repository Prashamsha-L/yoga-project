About YogaAI

YogaAI is your personal companion for learning and practicing yoga in a smarter way. Our platform combines guided tutorials, pose detection technology, and a comprehensive yoga library to help you improve your mind and body balance efficiently.

Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced practitioner, YogaAI provides step-by-step guidance, pose benefits, and daily wellness tracking to keep your yoga journey consistent and enjoyable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make yoga accessible to everyone, anytime and anywhere, by leveraging technology to provide interactive learning and real-time posture feedback. We believe in holistic well-being and aim to empower users to live healthier, more mindful lives.
